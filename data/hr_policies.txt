COMPANY HR POLICIES

LEAVE POLICY
Employees receive 21 days of annual leave per year. Leave resets on January 1st. Part-time employees get leave on a pro-rata basis. Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days.

SICK LEAVE POLICY
Employees get 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences longer than 3 consecutive days. Sick leave does not carry forward to the next year.

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
Employees can work from home up to 3 days per week. Core hours are 10 AM to 4 PM. WFH must be approved by the direct manager. Employees must be reachable during core hours.

MATERNITY AND PATERNITY LEAVE
Maternity leave is 26 weeks fully paid. Paternity leave is 2 weeks fully paid. Must notify HR at least 8 weeks before expected leave start date.

PERFORMANCE REVIEW POLICY
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year in June and December. Employees submit a self-evaluation form 2 weeks before the review. Ratings: Exceptional, Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement.

EXPENSE POLICY
Submit expense claims within 30 days using the Expensify app. Receipts required for all expenses above $25. Claims are reimbursed within 10 business days. Travel expenses: Economy class for flights under 6 hours.

PAYROLL POLICY
Payroll is processed on the 25th of each month. If the 25th falls on a weekend, payment is on the preceding Friday. Payslips are available on the HR portal 3 days before payment.

REMOTE WORK INTERNATIONAL POLICY
Employees can work remotely from another country for up to 30 days per year. Notify HR and manager at least 2 weeks in advance. Tax implications must be reviewed with Finance for stays over 14 days.

PROMOTION POLICY
Promotions are reviewed in June and December. Submit a self-evaluation form to HR at least 30 days before the review. Promotions require manager recommendation and HR approval.

REFERRAL BONUS POLICY
Employees receive $2000 for successfully referring a candidate who completes 6 months. The bonus is paid after the 6-month probation period ends. One referral bonus per quarter per employee.
